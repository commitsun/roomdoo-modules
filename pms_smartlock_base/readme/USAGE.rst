* Codes are generated automatically for reservations checking in within the
  next 24 hours. To issue them earlier, open the reservation and use
  **Generate Codes Now** in the *Smart Lock Codes* section.
* To give a guest their PIN, open the reservation (or **PMS ‣ Smart Locks ‣
  Codes**) and click **Reveal PIN**. The viewer shows the PIN followed by the
  keypad confirm key (e.g. ``#``) the guest must press after typing it. Each
  reveal is recorded in **PMS ‣ Smart Locks ‣ PIN Access Log**.
* Lock communication runs in the background through queue jobs. Inspect
  retries and errors from the **Sync Jobs** listed on each code.
